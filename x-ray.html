Settings & data notes
The X-Ray uses Finnhub for live quotes, company profiles, recommendations, company metrics and news when your plan allows them. It also tries Yahoo's public market-data endpoints through fallbacks for additional fundamentals. Free feeds sometimes omit fields or temporarily block browser requests; missing information is shown as unavailable rather than invented. The app separates data confidence (feed completeness) from investment confidence (clarity and consistency of the evidence).
